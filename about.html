Why we started The Online Tricks, and what we actually do before a deal reaches you.

Why We Started

The Online Tricks began as a simple idea: most "deal" content online is either fake discounts, expired links, or bots scraping random listings with no real check behind them. We wanted a place where a posted deal actually means what it says — the price is real, the link works, and the product is worth buying.

What We Do

Every day, we track pricing across Amazon, Flipkart, and other online retailers, and manually verify each deal before it's shared — on our website, our Telegram channel, and our other social platforms. If a deal expires or a link breaks, we take it down rather than leave it live for the sake of content.
